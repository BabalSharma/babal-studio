Privacy Policy

This website stores only the project and account information needed to manage client login, service orders, and admin workflow.

What data is collected

Name, email address, phone number, order details, and account authentication details through Firebase.

Why data is used

To create client accounts, save project orders, communicate about work, and manage service delivery through the admin dashboard.

Who can access it

Clients can access only their own account and orders. Administrative access is limited to authorized internal accounts and is not publicly exposed in site navigation.

Storage and protection

Data is stored using Firebase services protected by authentication and Firestore / Storage security rules.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact babalsharma.studio@gmail.com.
